Our mission for Fitness Club is to provide students at Saratoga High School with the proper knowledge of nutrition, help them achieve their athletic goals, and build them into healthier, stronger, and more athletic individuals. We plan to utilize the weight room as frequently as possible. Additionally, we plan to utilize virtual resources (Zoom, IGTV) to encourage students to try an array of workouts, build a healthier diet, and lead an overall healthier lifestyle.
